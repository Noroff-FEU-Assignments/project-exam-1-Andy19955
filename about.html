About The Guinea Pigs

We are two guinea pigs named Nala and Evangeline that want to share our adventures with people through our blog. Our story starts on the 18th of May when our humans bought us and brought us home with them. We would be thrilled if you would like to join us on our adventures that we write about on this blog!

We get to experience a lot of different things like car rides, playing with toys and obstacles, eating the fresh grass outside our appartment while expreiencing all the sounds and smells outside in the nature and a lot more. This is basically what we are writing about on this blog.

We try to post as frequent as possible, but we are sometimes very busy eating or taking naps. But don't worry. Our humans help us to write posts sometimes when we are busy with other stuff. They also know where all our hidouts, obstacles, toys etc. is bought, so feel free to send us a message on the "Contact" page if there's any question.

~ Nala and Evangeline
